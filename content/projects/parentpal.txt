### **Overview**
ParentPal is an empathetic, safety-aligned AI parenting assistant grounded entirely in HealthyChildren.org (American Academy of Pediatrics). It delivers reliable, age-appropriate guidance for new parents.

---

### **Problem**
Parents are overwhelmed by contradictory online information. They need trusted, source-based advice—and AI models require strict guardrails to avoid unsafe recommendations.

---

### **Architecture Diagram (Custom GPT with Safety Layer)**
```mermaid
flowchart LR
  User --> GPT[Custom GPT]
  GPT --> Rules["System Prompt: Safety + AAP-only Sources"]
  Rules --> Healthy[HealthyChildren.org Articles]
  Healthy --> GPT
  GPT --> User
```

---

### **Tech Stack**
- OpenAI Custom GPT builder  
- Safety guardrails + structured prompts  
- HealthyChildren.org corpus  
- Retrieval constraints

---

### **Key Challenges**
- Enforcing strict guardrails for medical-adjacent topics  
- Designing empathetic yet factual responses  
- Ensuring citations map to approved AAP content  
- Maintaining consistency across age groups

---

### **What I Learned**
- How to design AI for safety-critical domains  
- How to structure system prompts with constraints  
- How to build trust through transparency  
- How empathy shapes UX in family-oriented tools
- Future enhancements
  - integration with web connectors for dynamic article retrieval, and improved contextual recall for ongoing parent–child discussions.
