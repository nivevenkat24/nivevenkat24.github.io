### **Overview**
Analyzed a dataset of 5,000+ sales records to identify revenue drivers and market trends across regions and product lines.

---

### **Problem**
Sales volume declined by 86.11% over the period. The business needed to investigate external market factors or internal issues and optimize inventory, pricing, and marketing to reverse the drop.

---

### **Key Findings**
- Sales Forecasting: Significant decline in sales volume (-86.11%); immediate intervention required.  
- Customer Behavior: Top markets are Australia, UK, and Indiaâ€”prioritize marketing spend here.  
- Market Trends: Preferences vary by region (e.g., Australia favors 50% Dark Bites, UK prefers Peanut Butter Cubes).

---

### **Tech Stack**
- Python  
- Pandas (data cleaning and analysis)  
- Seaborn (visualization)

---

### **Impact**
- Built a data cleaning pipeline to fix currency and date issues, improving data quality end to end.  
- Created visualizations of monthly trends and top products to guide inventory decisions.  
- Automated data acquisition and reporting, reducing manual analysis time.
