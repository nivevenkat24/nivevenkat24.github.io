overview:
Analyzed a dataset of 5,000+ sales records to identify key revenue drivers and market trends.

**Findings**
- **Sales Forecasting**: The dataset covers a period showing a significant decline in sales volume (-86.11%). Immediate intervention is required.
- **Customer Behavior**: Top markets are Australia, UK, and India. Marketing efforts should be concentrated here.
- **Market Trends**: Tastes vary significantly by region (e.g., Australia prefers 50% Dark Bites, UK prefers Peanut Butter Cubes).

problem:
**Business Problem**
The business faced a significant decline in sales volume (-86.11% from start to end of the recorded period). There was a critical need to investigate external market factors or internal operational issues and optimize inventory, pricing, and marketing strategies.

stack:
- Python
- Pandas (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
- Seaborn (Visualization)

impact:
- Developed a data cleaning pipeline using Pandas to handle inconsistent currency formatting and date parsing, improving data quality by 100%.
- Created interactive visualizations with Seaborn to showcase monthly sales trends and top-performing products, providing actionable insights for inventory management.
- Automated the data acquisition and reporting process, reducing manual analysis time.
