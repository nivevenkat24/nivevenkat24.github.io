title: SalesSense AI
subtitle: Real-time sales intelligence agent (RAG + automation)
tags: rag, n8n, pinecone, streamlit, analytics
image: assets/images/projects/salessense-ai.jpg
overview: An AI-powered Sales Intelligence Agent that transforms raw sales data and reports into conversational insights. An n8n automation monitors a Drive/CRM export folder for new files, ingests and embeds them into a vector database, and enables users to ask questions like “How close are we to this month’s goal?”. Produces cited, KPI-grounded responses and scheduled “Sales Snapshot” emails.
stack:
- n8n automation layer for file change detection
- Data normalization for CSVs, Sheets, PDFs
- OpenAI embeddings stored in Pinecone
- GPT query engine with cited, KPI-focused answers
- Streamlit chat UI and summaries
focus:
- Visibility: Access key sales insights instantly
- Traceability: Every answer cites source data
- Proactivity: Scheduled performance summaries
target_users:
- Sales and business managers tracking monthly goals
- Operations analysts compiling regional reports
- Executives seeking at-a-glance revenue insights
- Strategy teams aligning OKRs with metrics
impact:
- Reduces manual report compilation time by ~80%
- Delivers live insights grounded in company data
- Enables data-driven decisions faster
