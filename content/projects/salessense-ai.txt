### **Overview**

A RAG-powered AI agent that analyzes sales files, embeds them into a vector database, and enables conversational analysis of KPIs, performance trends, and competitor insights.
---

### **Problem**

Sales ops teams deal with scattered PDFs, Excel files, and decks. Answering even basic questions about growth or retention requires time-consuming manual work.

### **Architecture Diagram (Sales RAG System)**

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[Drive Folder] --> B[n8n Ingestion Pipeline]
    B --> C[Embedding Pipeline]
    C --> D[Vector DB]
    D --> E[LLM Answer Engine]
    E --> F[Chat UI]
```
---
### **Tech Stack**
- Python
- n8n automation
- Pinecone
- LangChain
- GPT models
- Streamlit UI

---

### **Key Challenges**
- Normalizing unstructured sales files
- Ensuring accurate KPI extraction
- Updating embeddings as new files arrive
- Avoiding hallucinations when data is missing

---

### **What I Learned**
- How to operationalize data ingestion for business users
- How RAG behaves with structured vs. unstructured data
- How to design explainability into business AI tools
- How to balance transparency with speed
