overview:
A data-driven business plan for **ScentScout**, a platform designed to help fragrance enthusiasts find the best deals on luxury perfumes by aggregating listings from high-volume sellers.

**The Solution**
A curated marketplace that vets sellers, tracks price drops, and guarantees authenticity.
- **Vets Sellers**: Only lists items from verified hubs (Dallas, NJ).
- **Price Tracking**: Alerts for "Price Mistakes" on top brands.
- **Revenue Model**: Affiliate commissions + Premium subscriptions ($5/mo).

problem:
**The Problem**
- **High Retail Markups**: Luxury perfumes have massive markups in retail.
- **Fragmented Grey Market**: Affordable authentic perfumes exist on eBay, but finding trustworthy sellers is difficult.
- **Opaque Pricing**: Prices vary by 50%+ depending on the seller and location.

stack:
- Business Strategy
- Market Analysis
- Revenue Modeling
- Operational Planning

impact:
- Designed a scalable aggregator model with low inventory risk.
- Identified key logistics hubs (Dallas, NJ, Brooklyn) for supply partnerships.
- Created a 3-phase operational roadmap from MVP to international expansion.
