### **Overview**
Data-driven business plan for **ScentScout**, a platform that helps fragrance enthusiasts find the best deals on luxury perfumes by aggregating listings from high-volume sellers.

---

### **Problem**
- High retail markups on luxury perfumes  
- Fragmented grey market where authentic deals exist but trustworthy sellers are hard to find  
- Opaque pricing that varies by 50%+ depending on seller and location

---

### **Solution**
A curated marketplace that vets sellers, tracks price drops, and guarantees authenticity.  
- Vets Sellers: Only lists items from verified hubs (Dallas, NJ).  
- Price Tracking: Alerts for “Price Mistakes” on top brands.  
- Revenue Model: Affiliate commissions plus $5/mo premium subscriptions.

---

### **Tech Stack / Approach**
- Business strategy  
- Market analysis  
- Revenue modeling  
- Operational planning

---

### **Impact**
- Designed a scalable aggregator model with low inventory risk  
- Identified key logistics hubs (Dallas, NJ, Brooklyn) for supply partnerships  
- Created a three-phase operational roadmap from MVP to international expansion
